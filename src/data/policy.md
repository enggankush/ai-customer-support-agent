# Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-25

## Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive refund policy for our e-commerce platform. All refund decisions are made based on the following rules and customer circumstances.

---

## Refund Rules

### Rule 1: Standard 30-Day Refund Window

**Status:** ✅ Approval Criterion  
**Condition:** Any item refund request must be made within 30 days of purchase date.  
**Details:**

- Requests made after 30 days will be denied unless special circumstances apply
- Premium customers may request manual review for requests 31-60 days after purchase
- All approved refunds in this window are subject to condition evaluation

**Example Scenario:** Customer purchases item on June 1, can request refund until June 30.

---

### Rule 2: Damaged or Defective Items

**Status:** ✅ Approval Criterion  
**Condition:** Items received damaged, defective, or with manufacturing issues are eligible for refund regardless of unopened status.  
**Details:**

- Customer must provide description of damage
- High-value items may require photo documentation
- Damage must have occurred during shipping or manufacturing
- Refund includes full price and shipping

**Example Scenario:** Wireless headphones arrive with audio defect → APPROVED (within 30 days).

---

### Rule 3: Digital Products Cannot Be Refunded

**Status:** ❌ Denial Criterion  
**Condition:** Software, eBooks, digital downloads, and subscriptions are non-refundable once accessed.  
**Details:**

- This applies to all digital goods regardless of purchase date
- Includes: software licenses, digital courses, music, movies, ebooks
- Streaming subscriptions (30+ day subscriptions) may allow one refund within 7 days
- No exceptions for "change of mind"

**Example Scenario:** Customer buys Microsoft Office 365 → DENIED (digital product).

---

### Rule 4: Gift Cards Cannot Be Refunded

**Status:** ❌ Denial Criterion  
**Condition:** Gift cards and store credits are non-refundable in all circumstances.  
**Details:**

- Applies to all gift card denominations and types
- Once issued/purchased, no refunds are available
- Lost or stolen gift cards may be replaced once with proof of purchase
- This is an absolute rule with no exceptions

**Example Scenario:** Customer buys $50 Amazon gift card → DENIED (gift card).

---

### Rule 5: Opened Personal Care Items Cannot Be Refunded

**Status:** ❌ Denial Criterion (with exceptions)  
**Condition:** Beauty, hygiene, and personal care items that have been opened cannot be refunded for health and safety reasons.  
**Details:**

- Applies to: shampoo, conditioner, skincare, makeup, health supplements
- Sealed items may be refunded if defective
- Opened items qualify for refund only if proven allergic reaction (medical documentation)
- Allergic reactions require medical report

**Example Scenario:** Customer opens shampoo and has reaction → APPROVED with medical proof.  
**Counter Example:** Customer wants to return opened shampoo for "not working well" → DENIED.

---

### Rule 6: Size/Fit Issues for Clothing and Footwear

**Status:** ✅ Approval Criterion  
**Condition:** Clothing and footwear items can be refunded if size is incorrect or fit issues are documented.  
**Details:**

- Items must be unworn with original tags attached
- Color/style changes are refundable within 30 days
- Customer must provide reason (too small, too large, wrong color)
- Free return shipping is provided

**Example Scenario:** Running shoes purchased in wrong size → APPROVED (unworn, within 30 days).

---

### Rule 7: Already Refunded Orders Cannot Be Refunded Again

**Status:** ❌ Denial Criterion  
**Condition:** Orders that have already received a refund are not eligible for additional refunds.  
**Details:**

- Full or partial refunds cannot be duplicated
- Store credit cannot be re-refunded to customer account
- If customer disputes a previous refund, must contact support directly
- Transaction history is reviewed to prevent duplicate refunds

**Example Scenario:** Customer already received refund on April 1 → DENIED if requesting again.

---

### Rule 8: Premium Customer Manual Review

**Status:** ⏳ Pending Review Criterion  
**Condition:** Premium members are eligible for manual review of borderline refund cases.  
**Details:**

- Applies to decisions that fall between approval and denial
- Manual review by human agent takes 24-48 hours
- Premium members get priority support
- Outside 30-day window requests: premium customers have 60-day consideration
- Premium members receive better outcomes on edge cases

**Example Scenario:** Premium customer requests refund 35 days after purchase → PENDING_REVIEW.

---

### Rule 9: High-Value Items Require Verification

**Status:** ⚠️ Conditional Approval  
**Condition:** Items over $200 require verification before refund processing.  
**Details:**

- Must have photos of item condition
- May require return shipping at customer cost if not defective
- Processing time extended to 7-10 business days for verification
- Items over $500 may require expert evaluation

**Example Scenario:** $300 gaming monitor with dead pixels → APPROVED after photo verification.

---

### Rule 10: Unused Items with Original Packaging

**Status:** ✅ Approval Criterion  
**Condition:** Completely unused items in original, undamaged packaging can be refunded within 30 days.  
**Details:**

- Item must be unopened and original packaging intact
- No signs of use or testing
- All original accessories and documentation must be included
- Refund processing time: 3-5 business days

**Example Scenario:** Laptop stand never opened, customer changed mind → APPROVED (within 30 days).

---

### Rule 11: Defective After 30 Days

**Status:** ⏳ Conditional (Limited Coverage)  
**Condition:** Items that become defective after 30 days may qualify for warranty replacement rather than refund.  
**Details:**

- Must be within manufacturer warranty period (typically 1-2 years)
- Refund not provided, but replacement or repair is available
- Electronics have 12-month manufacturer warranty
- Furniture/appliances follow manufacturer terms

**Example Scenario:** USB cable stops working after 2 months → REPLACEMENT under warranty.

---

### Rule 12: International Orders and Returns

**Status:** ⚠️ Special Conditions  
**Condition:** International orders may have modified refund terms due to customs and logistics.  
**Details:**

- Returns from international locations incur return shipping at customer cost
- Refunds may be reduced by return shipping and customs processing fees
- Processing time: 10-15 business days for international refunds
- Some countries have import restrictions on returns

**Example Scenario:** Customer in Canada returns item → APPROVED with $25 deduction for return shipping.

## Appeals and Escalations

- Customers can appeal any refund denial within 14 days
- Premium customers get expedited appeal review (48 hours)
- Appeals are reviewed by a different agent/manager
- Final decision is binding after appeal review

---

## Processing Timeline

- **Standard Refunds:** 3-5 business days
- **High-Value Items:** 7-10 business days (verification required)
- **Manual Reviews:** 24-48 hours additional processing
- **Replacement/Warranty:** 10-15 business days
